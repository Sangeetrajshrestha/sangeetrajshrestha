Data Resources

Kaggle.com

Kaggle is a data science community and platform that hosts competitions, provides datasets, and offers a collaborative environment for machine learning enthusiasts to solve real-world problems and showcase their skills.

Data.gov

Data.gov is a centralized online repository that provides access to a vast collection of government datasets, empowering users to explore, analyze, and utilize valuable public data for research, decision-making, and fostering transparency in governance.

Google Datasets Search

Google Datasets Search is a search engine designed to help researchers and data scientists discover and access a wide range of publicly available datasets. It indexes datasets from various domains and provides metadata information, facilitating efficient data exploration, analysis, and collaboration in the pursuit of knowledge and insights.
